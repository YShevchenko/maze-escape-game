# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 3, 2026**

## Introduction

Heldig Lab ("we", "our", or "us") operates mobile game applications (the "Apps"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Apps.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **None.** Our games do not require registration, login, or any personal information from users.

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Local Game Data:** Your game scores, preferences, and progress are stored locally on your device only.
- **No Analytics:** We do not collect analytics, usage statistics, or tracking data.
- **No Personal Data:** We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information.

## How We Use Information

- **Local Storage Only:** All game data (high scores, settings, achievements) is stored locally on your device using the device's secure storage (SharedPreferences on Android, UserDefaults on iOS).
- **No Server Communication:** Our games do not send any data to external servers.
- **No Third-Party Services:** We do not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Data Storage and Security

- **Local Only:** All data remains on your device and is never transmitted.
- **Your Control:** You can delete all game data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in your device settings.
- **No Backups:** We do not back up or sync your data to cloud services.

## Children's Privacy

Our games do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. Since we do not collect any personal information at all, our Apps are safe for children to use.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Delete all game data by uninstalling the app
- Clear app data through your device settings
- Use the app without providing any personal information

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
